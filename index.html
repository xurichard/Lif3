LIF3 is your own personal, remote bioreactor

It allows you to access and control your personal bioreactor cube in space on Earth

Cloud Compute your data

We gather your data and store it in the cloud to free up local disk space. Cloud computations are also an option for an extra fee.

Ease of Access

To run your experiment in space, you can simply tell us what tools to place in your box and how long to run it. Then simply monitor your experiment from your account and check on the return data stream.

Live support

An astronaut will always be on board the international space station to monitor your bioreactor constantly.


How will you use LIF3?

Pick your tools, sit back, and watch the data roll in.

Run your experiment in space

Any experiment (within the limits of our bioreactor) from any location on Earth with wifi connection. Your commands will automatically be transferred to the ISS and data will be returned to all locations accessing it, all in real time.

  • See our Github

Access your data in real time

Since all the data gathered is transferred through the cloud, you can access your data from anywhere through this web interface. Additionally, we are implementing Intel's IoT Cloud-based Analytics so that we can assure simultaneous data access.
